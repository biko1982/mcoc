Introduction

This is a first draft for an alliance-webpage. You will find our vision, a few rules here and the way the alliance is managed. In time I hope to put more information on here, and links to sites that are useful

Vision

Have fun, enjoy and play the game and join the stuff you like.

Rules

  1. Life comes first
    But MCOC a close second ;-)
  2. Summoners LVL 20 minimum
    Due to the content we aim to play, we need 20+. Sorry for those who aren't there yet, we hope to see you soon!
  3. Play the game
    If possible: login at least a few times a week
    If not possible: see number 4
  4. Communicate
    A lot is possible, and we have rule 1 as first rule for a reason. But not showing up without a reason means: Exit
  5. Only start what you can finish
    If a linked node is to buffed for you to handle: wait until you go in
    If you start an AQ or AW, make sure there are enough people to see it through to the end.
  6. Maximum 1 AW a week.
    Preferably enlistment on Friday, fighting in the weekend
  7. No Donations
    We don't need your money, we want your enthusiasm.

Managment ;-)

  • Active members who are at least level 40 are eligble to become an officer.
    An exception to this is Redtiger271, who is an honorary officer and founder of our alliance.
  • Leader and officers can start AQ and AW. Please do so only if you can comply with rule 5.
